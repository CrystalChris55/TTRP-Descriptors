description=Expanding upon the many, multiple already existing Roleplay exclaims mod out there. This mod aims to be the definitive and final answer to immersive roleplaying descriptors for RP servers. The full list of features are below:
description=
description=[h1]Features:[/h1]
description=[list]
description=[*] Sandbox configurable options, allowing category usage to be disabled for non-admin players, or for scenes to entirely be disabled for non-admin players to allow for a fully curated and immersive experience on RP servers. (By default, all are set to disabled.)
description=[*] Fully custom made UI to spawn all of the objects/exclaims/props in, crisp and clean.
description=[*] Halo text above the player's head that displays the name of the scene or RP descriptor, on not only exclaims and questions, but all of the items spawned from the menu.
description=[*] Floating and ground variants of all the familiar question and exclaims that you know and love.
description=[/list]
